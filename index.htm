ABOUT SCHOOL

G.D. Goenka Public School at Dakshineswar, Kolkata is a promising signature in the field of quality education. It is an English medium co-educational CBSE School. Keeping in mind our motto: “Higher Stronger Brighter”, we give our little ones the very best, each and every day.


FACULTY

The real strength and the character of any educational institute is determined by its faculty. G.D. Goenka Public School, Dakshineswar handpick the best of the teachers who act as facilitators between the child and the world. Our children have privilege of being guided by a team of highly qualified, experienced and dedicated teachers. Teacher is the one who plants seed of knowledge in the children that grows forever . Apart from teaching academics, the teachers foster their experience with them in the classroom. We provide ample opportunities to the teachers for their academic development. Interactive workshops for all the teachers are organized in the areas of emotional, social, and professional relevance. We also organize excursion trips to keep them rejuvenate and full of zip.


OUR MISSION

It is for us to strategize school activities so that no opportunity to build advantage is missed. In our mission, each academic or extra experience, each small interaction must build into a larger mosaic of understanding. Our constant aim is to raise awareness, broaden horizons, inspire debate and inculcate values that are sterling in quality.
